# Product Requirements Document: Volt Digital Landing Page

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** January 2026
**Status:** Draft

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Volt Digital Landing Page is a conversion-focused website designed to attract small business owners seeking digital solutions. The primary goal is to establish brand credibility, clearly communicate the value proposition, and collect email addresses from interested prospects for nurturing campaigns.

---

## 2. Product Overview

### 2.1 Vision
Create a professional, modern landing page that positions Volt Digital as a trusted digital solutions provider for small business owners while maximizing email capture for lead nurturing.

### 2.2 Product Description
A single-page website that introduces Volt Digital's services, demonstrates value through social proof and results, and funnels visitors toward email signup with minimal friction.

---

## 3. Target Audience

**Primary Persona:** Small Business Owner
- Age: 28-55
- Business Stage: Established (1-10 years in business)
- Pain Points: Limited tech expertise, time constraints, budget limitations, need for growth
- Goals: Increase online visibility, generate leads, streamline operations, improve customer engagement
- Tech Comfort: Moderate (uses social media and email, less comfortable with technical implementation)

**Secondary Persona:** Business Manager/Marketing Lead
- Tasked with improving digital presence
- Looking for affordable, reliable agency partnerships

---

## 4. Goals & Success Metrics

### 4.1 Primary Goals
1. **Email Capture:** Collect 100+ qualified email addresses per month
2. **Brand Awareness:** Establish Volt Digital as a credible digital solutions provider
3. **Lead Quality:** Generate leads ready for discovery calls or consultations

### 4.2 Success Metrics
- **Conversion Rate:** 8-12% of visitors submit email (benchmark: 2-5% for average landing pages)
- **Page Load Time:** < 3 seconds on mobile, < 2 seconds on desktop
- **Mobile Performance:** 90+ Lighthouse score
- **Bounce Rate:** < 40%
- **Email Signups:** 100+ per month minimum
- **Click-Through Rate (CTA):** 15%+ for primary email signup button

---

## 5. Key Features & Page Sections

### 5.1 Navigation Header
**Elements:**
- Volt Digital logo (top-left)
- Sticky navigation bar with links:
  - Services
  - About
  - Contact
  - Sign-up CTA button (email signup)
- Mobile hamburger menu
- Navigation remains visible when scrolling

**Behavior:**
- Header background becomes semi-transparent with scroll
- Logo links back to top
- Sign-up button visible at all times

### 5.2 Hero Section
**Elements:**
- Bold, compelling headline: "Digital Solutions Built for Small Business Growth"
- Subheadline: "Expert marketing, web, and technology services to help your business thrive"
- High-quality background image or video (abstract/professional aesthetic)
- Primary CTA button: "Get Started - Free Consultation" (link to email form)
- Trust badges (optional): "Trusted by 100+ Small Businesses"

**Copy Focus:**
- Speaks directly to small business owner pain points
- Emphasizes simplicity, affordability, and results

### 5.3 Value Proposition Section (3-Pillar Cards)
**Elements:** Three equal-width cards presenting core service areas

**Card 1: Digital Marketing**
- Icon/illustration
- Headline: "Grow Your Reach"
- Description: SEO, social media, content marketing, and paid advertising tailored to your budget
- Link: "Learn More"

**Card 2: Web & Design**
- Icon/illustration
- Headline: "Stand Out Online"
- Description: Professional websites, e-commerce, and design that converts visitors to customers
- Link: "Learn More"

**Card 3: Business Solutions**
- Icon/illustration
- Headline: "Streamline Your Operations"
- Description: Automation, CRM setup, customer management systems, and tech integrations
- Link: "Learn More"

**Behavior:**
- Cards stack vertically on mobile
- Hover effects on desktop (shadow, slight lift)
- Links may expand content or scroll to detailed sections

### 5.4 Social Proof Section
**Elements:**
- Client logo gallery (5-8 logos)
- Short testimonial quotes from small business clients
- "Trusted by X+ businesses" statement
- Star rating (4.8/5.0 based on Google/Trustpilot reviews)

**Content:**
- Real logos from actual clients
- 2-3 brief testimonials (50-100 words each) highlighting specific results
- Face photos of testimonial authors if possible

### 5.5 Results & Impact Section
**Elements:**
- 3-4 Key Metrics displayed prominently:
  - Average ROI improvement (e.g., "3x ROI increase")
  - Customer satisfaction (e.g., "98% Client Satisfaction")
  - Years of combined experience (e.g., "50+ years of expertise")
  - Projects completed (e.g., "500+ successful projects")
- Brief explanation of how Volt Digital delivers results

**Design:**
- Use counter animations for numbers (0 → final number)
- Bold typography
- Icon accompaniment

### 5.6 Services Deep-Dive Section
**Elements:**
- Expandable service categories or tabbed interface
- Service spotlight cards featuring:
  - Service name and icon
  - 2-3 sentence description
  - Key benefits (bulleted)
  - "View Case Study" link

**Services Featured:**
1. SEO & Content Marketing
2. Social Media Management
3. Website Development
4. E-Commerce Solutions
5. Marketing Automation
6. Brand Development

**Behavior:**
- Tabs/accordion expand to show more details
- Can include case study previews or mini-galleries

### 5.7 Case Studies / Results Gallery Section
**Elements:**
- 2-3 featured case studies displayed in a carousel or grid
- Each case study includes:
  - Client industry/business type
  - Challenge statement (1 sentence)
  - Solution provided (2 sentences)
  - Key metrics/results (e.g., "150% increase in leads, 8-week timeline")
  - "Read Full Case Study" link

**Design:**
- Card-based layout with background image or color
- Mobile: Stack vertically
- Carousel on desktop with next/prev controls

### 5.8 Why Choose Volt Digital Section
**Elements:**
- 4-5 key differentiators:
  - "Affordable & Transparent Pricing" - No hidden fees
  - "Dedicated Account Manager" - Personal support
  - "Proven Track Record" - Real results for small businesses
  - "Quick Implementation" - Get started in days, not months
  - "Education & Training" - We teach you alongside execution

**Design:**
- Icon + headline + 1-2 sentence description format
- Organized in a grid or timeline

### 5.9 FAQ Section
**Elements:**
- 6-8 collapsible FAQ items addressing common objections:
  - "How much do your services cost?"
  - "What's the typical timeline to see results?"
  - "Do you work with businesses in my industry?"
  - "What if I'm not tech-savvy?"
  - "How do you measure success?"
  - "What makes Volt Digital different?"
  - "Can I cancel anytime?"
  - "Do you offer support after project completion?"

**Behavior:**
- Click to expand/collapse
- Smooth animation
- Only one FAQ open at a time (optional)

### 5.10 Email Signup Section (Secondary CTA)
**Elements:**
- Headline: "Ready to Accelerate Your Growth?"
- Subheadline: "Join 500+ small business owners getting digital marketing tips and exclusive offers"
- Email input field
- Submit button: "Get Free Consultation"
- Checkbox: "I'd like to receive marketing tips and updates" (optional pre-check)

**Design:**
- Soft background color or gradient
- Clean, minimal form (email field + button)
- Privacy note: "We'll never spam. Unsubscribe anytime."

**Behavior:**
- Form validation: Email must be valid format
- Success message on submission: "Check your email for next steps"
- Optional: Email stored in CRM/email marketing platform

### 5.11 Footer
**Elements:**
- Company info (address, phone, email)
- Quick links:
  - Services
  - About
  - Blog
  - Privacy Policy
  - Terms of Service
- Social media icons (LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter/X, Instagram)
- Copyright notice
- Secondary email signup prompt (optional)

**Design:**
- Dark background with light text
- Organized in columns
- Links underline on hover

## 6. User Experience Flow

### 6.1 Primary User Flow (Email Signup)
1. **Awareness:** User arrives on hero section
2. **Interest:** Scrolls through value props and social proof
3. **Consideration:** Explores services and case studies
4. **Decision:** Encounters email signup section
5. **Action:** Submits email for consultation or lead magnet
6. **Follow-up:** Confirmation email sent with next steps

### 6.2 Alternative Flows
- User clicks "Learn More" on service cards → Scrolls to detailed service section
- User clicks testimonial → Expands full testimonial or case study
- User clicks "Read Full Case Study" → Links to detailed case study page (future)

## 7. Technical Requirements

### 7.1 Platform & Stack
- **Frontend:** Next.js / React (component-based, server-side rendering for SEO)
- **Hosting:** Vercel or similar (fast, optimized for Next.js)
- **Email Service:** Mailchimp, HubSpot, or ConvertKit for email capture
- **Form Handling:** EmailJS or Formspree for direct email submissions
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics 4 + Hotjar for heatmaps

### 7.2 Performance Requirements
- Page load time: < 3 seconds (Lighthouse)
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Lighthouse scores: 90+ across Performance, Accessibility, Best Practices, SEO
- Core Web Vitals: LCP < 2.5s, FID < 100ms, CLS < 0.1

### 7.3 Browser & Device Support
- Desktop: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest 2 versions)
- Mobile: iOS Safari, Chrome (latest 2 versions)
- Tablets: Full responsive support

### 7.4 Integrations
- Google Analytics tracking
- Email marketing platform (form submission → email list)
- Optional: Chatbot widget (Drift, Intercom) for engagement
- Optional: Calendar integration for booking consultations

### 7.5 SEO Requirements
- Meta descriptions for all pages
- Open Graph tags for social sharing
- Schema.org markup (Organization, LocalBusiness, BreadcrumbList)
- Mobile-optimized (Mobile-First Indexing)
- Sitemap.xml

## 8. Design Requirements

### 8.1 Visual Style
- **Color Palette:**
  - Primary: Electric Blue or Teal (#0066FF or #00D9FF)
  - Secondary: Dark Navy (#1A2332)
  - Accent: Orange or Green (#FF6B35 or #2ECC71)
  - Neutral: Light Gray (#F5F5F5), White (#FFFFFF)

- **Typography:**
  - Headlines: Modern, bold sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Poppins)
  - Body: Clean, readable sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Open Sans)
  - Font hierarchy: H1 (36-48px), H2 (28-36px), Body (16px), Small (14px)

- **Imagery:**
  - Professional stock photos or custom illustrations
  - Consistent visual language
  - Diverse representation of business owners

### 8.2 Component Design
- Consistent button styles (primary, secondary, tertiary)
- Card components with hover effects
- Smooth transitions and animations (200-300ms)
- Proper spacing and whitespace
- Accessibility: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance

### 8.3 Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: 320px - 768px
- Tablet: 768px - 1024px
- Desktop: 1024px+

## 9. Email Capture Strategy

### 9.1 Signup Methods
1. **Hero Section CTA Button** → Scrolls to signup form
2. **Dedicated Signup Section** → Primary form with benefit statement
3. **Exit-Intent Popup** (optional) → Triggered when user scrolls to exit
4. **Footer Signup** → Secondary opportunity

### 9.2 Lead Magnet (Future Enhancement)
- Free resource: "Small Business Digital Marketing Checklist" (downloadable PDF)
- Gated behind email capture
- Delivered immediately after signup

### 9.3 Email Nurture Sequence
- **Email 1 (Immediate):** Welcome + lead magnet delivery
- **Email 2 (Day 2):** Case study or success story
- **Email 3 (Day 5):** Offer free consultation or audit
- **Email 4 (Day 10):** Educational content + FAQ

---

## 10. Accessibility Requirements

- **WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance:**
  - Color contrast ratios ≥ 4.5:1 for text
  - Keyboard navigation support (Tab through all interactive elements)
  - Alt text for all images
  - Form labels properly associated with inputs
  - Semantic HTML structure

- **Screen Reader Support:**
  - Proper heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3)
  - ARIA labels for interactive components
  - Form validation messages announced
